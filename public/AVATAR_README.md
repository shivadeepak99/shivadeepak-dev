# Avatar

The hero shows your avatar at the top-left, before the green "Available" indicator.

## How to set yours

1. Generate (or pick) an anime-style boy avatar — square crop works best.
2. Resize to **256 × 256** PNG (smaller is fine, larger wastes bandwidth).
3. Save it as **`public/avatar.png`**.

That's it. The site auto-loads it.

## If `avatar.png` is missing

The hero falls back to a gradient ring with your initials (`SD`). No errors, just degraded look.

## Recommended generators

- **NovelAI / Stable Diffusion** — full control over style
- **PicLumen / Bing Image Creator** — free anime-style generators
- **Leonardo.ai** — paid, higher quality
- Or commission one on Fiverr (~$5–15) for something distinctive

## Style tips

- **Dark background or transparent** — matches the site's dark theme
- **Centered face/portrait** — works in a small circle
- **Slightly cool tones** (purple/blue) — pairs with the accent color
- Avoid full-body shots — they look tiny in 48×48
